P2P eCommerce Website

I want to create a P2P eCommerce website that is easy to use, secure and fast for both sellers and buyers. I want to use peer-to-peer networking to distribute access to webpages. I want to use decentralized networks so that there are no central points of failure and no single company can shut down the entire network.

This website would take Card, Bitcoin, and Mobile Payment. Payments may sit in a 3rd party account until payments and goods are shipped and then the transfer will be completed. This way sellers get paid and consumers get their goods. Sellers will have a 5 star rating system with an additional "Verified" status.

I want the website to be accessible from phones and tablets. I want users to be able to sell their products using an auction model where they set a price and enter in how many units of each item theyâ€™re selling. I want sellers to be able to track their sales over time and for buyers to be able to filter through products by price range and category.

All transactions will be anonymous and data will be encrypted for security and privacy reasons. As the hosting site for these business, we will take 3% of all revenue and may shift this based on specific items being sold.
